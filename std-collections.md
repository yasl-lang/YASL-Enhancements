# Collections Standard Library

The collections standard library will be called `collections`.

The following collections will be included:

- `set`
- `bytebuffer`

Some of the following may also be included:

- `frozen_set`
- `frozen_table`
- `frozen_multiset`
- `ordered_table`
- `ordered_set`
- `ordered_multiset`
- `str_list`
- `int_list`
- `float_list`
- `num_list`
- `rope`
- `multiset`

sets should support:
- `len s`
- `a | b`
- `a & b`
- `a ^ b`
- `a - b`
- `s->add(x)`
- `s->remove(x)`
- `s->copy()`
- `s->clear()`
